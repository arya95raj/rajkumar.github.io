About Me

I am an astrophysicist with a PhD in astronomy. My research focuses on X-ray binaries, accretion physics, and black hole spin estimation. I work on spectral and timing studies using data from space-based observatories like Swift and NuSTAR.

Research

I investigate the physical processes in accreting black holes and neutron stars, with particular interest in characterizing quasi-periodic oscillations, estimating black hole spins, and modeling accretion disk behavior.

Selected Publications

  • Black hole spin estimation of XTE J2012+381 using Swift/XRT and NuSTAR. (2024)
  • Study of type-B QPOs in Swift J1728.9-3613. (2023)
